About the School

Preparing Technologists for East Africa's Digital Economy

IAT School of Technology is a Nairobi-based institution dedicated to producing skilled, work-ready technology professionals. Our curriculum is developed with industry partners and taught by practitioners, so every graduate leaves with a portfolio of real project work.

Mission
To equip learners with practical technology skills that power careers, businesses and communities.
Vision
To be East Africa's most trusted school for hands-on, career-focused technology education.
Values
Excellence, integrity, innovation and community.

Admissions

How to Join IAT

Intakes in January, May and September. Admission is in three steps.

  1. 01

    Apply

    Submit your application by email or in person at our Nairobi campus. There is no application fee. You will need your KCSE certificate or equivalent, a national ID or passport, and one passport photograph.

  2. 02

    Enrol

    Receive your admission letter, pay the first instalment and attend orientation. Our admissions team will help you plan a class schedule around work and family commitments.

  3. 03

    Begin

    Start classes, join the student community and be matched with mentors and internship partners from your first term.

The September 2026 intake is open

Flexible payment plans and merit scholarships are available.
