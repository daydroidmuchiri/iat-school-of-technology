About Us

A Practical Tech School for East Africa's Digital Economy

IAT School of Technology is a Nairobi-based training institution dedicated to producing skilled, work-ready technology professionals. Our curriculum is designed with industry partners and taught by practitioners, so every student graduates with a portfolio of real projects — not just a certificate.

From our campus in Nairobi, we serve school leavers, career changers and working professionals across Kenya and the region with flexible day, evening and weekend classes.

🎯 Our Mission

To equip learners with practical technology skills that power careers, businesses and communities.

🌍 Our Vision

To be East Africa's most trusted school for hands-on, career-focused technology education.

🤝 Our Values

Excellence, integrity, innovation and ubuntu — we grow together.

Admissions

Joining IAT is Simple

Intakes in January, May and September. Apply in three steps.

  1. 1

    Apply

    Send your application by email or visit our Nairobi campus. No application fee.

  2. 2

    Submit Documents

    KCSE certificate or equivalent, national ID or passport, and one passport photo.

  3. 3

    Enrol

    Receive your admission letter, pay the first instalment and start your classes.
