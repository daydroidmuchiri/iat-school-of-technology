About Us

A Practical Tech School for East Africa's Digital Economy

IAT School of Technology is a Nairobi-based training institution dedicated to producing skilled, work-ready technology professionals. Our curriculum is designed with industry partners and taught by practitioners, so every student graduates with a portfolio of real projects — not just a certificate.

From our campus in Nairobi, we serve school leavers, career changers and working professionals across Kenya and the region with flexible day, evening and weekend classes.

Admissions

Your Journey Starts Here

Intakes in January, May and September. Three steps and you're in.

  1. 1

    You Apply

    Send your application by email or visit our Nairobi campus. No application fee. You'll need your KCSE certificate or equivalent, ID or passport, and one passport photo.

  2. 2

    You Get Ready

    Receive your admission letter, pay the first instalment and attend orientation. Our team helps you plan your schedule around work and life.

  3. 3

    We Connect

    Start classes, join the student community and get matched with mentors and internship partners from day one.
